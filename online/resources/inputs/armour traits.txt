types
three types can be found and worn at the same time
light armour up to gambeson
medium up to a set of mail
heavy up to plate with helm

light
doesnt restrict movement and slightly decreases all damage types

medium
doesnt restrict movement and decreases slashing damage

heavy
somewhat restricts movement and heavily decreases slashing and pierce damage

NB - critical hit will ignore armour damage decreases
